Trackers
Trackers online can collect a crazy amount of data on users, it's kinda messed up honestly. I was recently browsing the internet and wanted to see how many trackers there really where in a website. I visited a bunch of websites seaking information, a good majority of the websites I looked at had at least two or three trackers, some had as many as twenty.
IP Grabbers
IP Grabbers are annoying, there is no escaping them, they are in almost every single website you can visit. You can't fix the IP collection issue that easily but you can prevent yourself from clicking on links that let other users get your IP. That information can be trusted a little more within companies; you don't want that little annoying kid getting your IP address though.
Sketchy Websites
There are a ton of websites that have dangerous code on them, track you or steal your information. The internet truly has become a battleground of information and viruses, malware, etc. You need to protect yourself from these websites before something bad happens. The internet is always getting more and more powerful, but with that, the danger becomes greater and greater.
Anonymous Extension
The browser extension designed to help you stay safe with nothing in return. It sounds almost too good to be true. Anonymous Extension is completely open source on GitHub! Anonymous Extension blocks suspicious trackers and websites but never tracks you. This is because we believe in the internet but we also believe in security and privacy. We hate having people track us so we developed Anonymous Extension. We want to share this with you!
