RainyDais
RainyDais [rainydais.com] is a hobby project I commonly work on in my free time. It is a small, basic social media platform designed to be the least harmful social network available. It does almost everything any other social media platform does but just different. There are some limitations in comparison obviously.
Blooket Hack
The kgsensei Blooket hack [link here] was a hacking/automation project I created because I don't like learning stuff in school. Admittedly it's not the best Blooket hack and there are so many more now that it started to take off. For a while I was almost the only one. It got the most attention around the end of the school year in 2021 getting as much as 20,000 views in a little under one month. While it's no longer the top dog of Blooket hacks it is the longest running one and almost the first one.
Neo Photo
Neo Photo is an unreleased photo editor built with python. It is quite capable and I plan on releasing it in the future. You can do a ton with New Photo, including but not limited to, editing contrast, editing brightness, burring and gaussian blurring, changing sharpness, embossing, inverting and contouring images as well as greyscale and color booster features. It currently has JPEG and PNG support. Though it isn't a lot, I do this for fun, not really for anything else.
jdigitalserver
The jdigitalserver website [link here] is a small site I wrote when I was quite new to programming, when it was first released in 2017 there was a small live chat that me and my friends would use to talk during school. It had a few unblocked games on it too. Later in 2019/early 2020 I shut it down for inactivity as covid struck.
-- Fast Forward About a Year --
In the middle of 2021 I started high school. I went to a different school than all my friends and we where back in physical school instead of virtual school, suddenly the desire to communicate started once more; I began to reboot the server. After a little bit of work it went back online.
